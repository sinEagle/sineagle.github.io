观察可以发现
$$ \sum_{i=l}^{r} {a_i \times (r-i+1)} $$
$$ = \sum_{i=l}^{r} {a_i \times (r+1)} -
\sum_{i=l}^{r} {a_i \times i}$$
$$ = (r+1)\times\sum_{i=l}^{r} {a_i } -
\sum_{i=l}^{r} {a_i \times i} $$

这样就能用两个树状数组或线段树来维护了
